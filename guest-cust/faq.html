delivery questions

How long does delivery take? 
Our standard delivery time is typically 3-5 business days for West Malaysia and 7-9 business days for East Malaysia.Business days are Monday through Friday (excluding holidays).
Can I track my order? 
Absolutely! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email that allows you to monitor its progress.
What if my item doesn't arrive? 
If your item hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe, please contact our support team immediately. We'll investigate and resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
What delivery options do you offer? 
We just offer a standard shipping delivery options.

payment questions

What payment methods do you accept? 
We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers.
Is my payment information secure? 
Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to ensure that all transactions are secure and your personal information is protected.
What should I do if there's an error in my billing? 
If you notice any discrepancies in your billing, please reach out to our customer support team. We'll review the issue and make necessary corrections.
Are there any hidden fees when making a purchase? 
No, all fees are clearly stated during the checkout process. The total cost includes the item price, shipping, and any applicable taxes.
